Classic Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and saut√©.
  3. Add tomato sauce and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in cooked pasta, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Beat the egg and mix with milk.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Cook on a greased pan until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup and enjoy!
